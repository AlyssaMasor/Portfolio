Life is a Journey...

We are born on a path we didn't choose, and work our whole lives to find direction. What we lose sight of, is that the meaning is in the journey. We all come to the same place in the end. There is no need to rush. Rather, savor the journey, embrace the dead ends, learn from backtracking, but don't dwell on it. Be mindful of who is accompanying you, what loads you carry, and the memory of all the miles you have travelled...

We only get to take it once...
